Privacy Policy for Contestify - Contest Reminder

Effective Date: 18 June, 2024

Welcome to Contestify - Contest Reminder! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, transmit, and protect your data. This is a free app, supported by google ads using Google Admob.

Information We Collect

We collect and store the following information from our users:

  • Email Address
  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • User Provided Competitive Contest Username
  • Alarm Sound Selected by User

Information We Transmit to Servers

We transmit the following data to our servers:

  • Competitive Contest Username
  • Users' Website Preferences
  • Alarm and Timer Notification Details
  • Feedback Given by the User
  • Alarm Sound Selected by the User

Purpose of Data Collection and Transmission

All collected and transmitted data is used solely for integrating into the app and improving its performance. We do not use any personal information for our personal use. Specifically, we use the data to:

  • Provide and enhance the functionality of the app.
  • Personalize user experience based on preferences and feedback.
  • Manage and trigger alarms and notifications as set by the users.
  • Improve app performance and address any issues reported by users.

Data Deletion

Users can request account deletion by emailing us at dhanesh23122003@gmail.com. Please include your email address and any relevant information in your request.

Permissions

Our app requests the following permissions for the specified purposes:

  • INTERNET: To access the internet for data transmission and retrieval.
  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW: To display alerts over other apps.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To check network connectivity status.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To read user-selected alarm sounds.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To save user preferences and settings.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To send notifications for alarms and reminders.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: To reschedule alarms after device reboot.
  • USE_EXACT_ALARM: To set precise alarm times.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: To schedule alarms accurately.
  • USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT: To ensure alarms can show in full-screen mode.
  • WAKE_LOCK: To keep the device awake for alarm notifications.
